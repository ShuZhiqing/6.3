About Us

Vicsmile is developed by Team QuadCore as an Industry Experience Project in the Faculty of Information Technology at Monash University, Australia. This Website is intended for educational purpose only.

This website is intended for use by people who are newly migrated to Victoria, some of them are but not limited to refugees and asylum seekers, foreign workers, international students, and new permanent residents. This website is developed to provide general access to dental health services in Victoria. Users can search public dental clinics, foods, and view informative materials and learn more about dental health.
