3. How to search for Public Dental Clinics
In the search bar you can type the name of the suburb your prefer or your current suburb to the the nearest dental clinics. If you are unsure of the location use the current location button to automatically fill in the location details.
Select the preferred languages to find the dentists who speaks .You can adjust the distance of the clinic from your location. Then choose the group and click on the Search Button.
By default the clinics nearest and having less waiting time are displayed first in the list. The results can be sorted using the filters.
4. About waiting times for public dental clinics
The waiting times are approximate time in months you have to wait for your turn for the dental treatment in the public dental clinics. The waiting time differs from one clinic to another and only after assessing your condition you may be placed general waiting list. A patient can be on the waiting list for only one community agency at a time, although you may transfer without penalty and will retain their original Date Placed on List. The waiting list is updated yearly based on publicly available data from DHSV. It is advised for you to contact the clinic to enquire about the current waiting time.
5. What to do in case of an emergency?
In case of an emergency or based on the priority, arrangements will be made for immediate treatment and not based on the waiting list. You may contact your nearest public dental clinic for assistance in case of an emergency. You can locate the nearest dental clinic using the search function available in the website.
6. Are we associated with these services ?
We are not associated with any of the service providers listed in the website or the Dental Clinics. We provide the information combined from various publically available open data.
