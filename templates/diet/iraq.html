Iraq

Average daily Iraqi nutrition intake has high to very high amount of protein, vitamin B12, and phosphorus. Unlike other countries where the high amount of these nutrients comes from animal products. Iraqis get these amounts from high consumption of fruit, vegetables and grains. Since the political instability from the 1990s until now, the nutritional quality of food average Iraqis take daily has decreased overtime. They need to consume more food source that have high content of calcium, vitamin A, B12, and C.

Food source rich in:
  • Calcium: milk, buttermilk, beans, lentils
  • Vitamin A: butter, cheese, egg, sweet potato, squash, carrot
  • Vitamin B12: milk, egg
  • Vitamin C: broccoli, spinach, orange
